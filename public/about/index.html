About

Jedy Sukandra is a Los Angeles-based visual creative and assistant editor working across photography, film, and television. After spending six years capturing the energy of live concerts and events as a Camera Operator, he transitioned into post-production, where he now creates and manages Avid Media Composer workflows. His background spans feature documentaries, scripted television, and reality TV, combining a deep technical understanding of video recording systems with the precision and efficiency required for long-form storytelling.
