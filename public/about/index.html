About

Jedy Sukandra is a Los Angeles-based lead assistant editor and visual artist working across photography, design, film, and television.

After six years as a camera operator capturing live concerts and events, he transitioned into post-production, where he now builds and manages Avid Media Composer editorial workflows.

His credits span feature documentaries, scripted television, and reality shows for Netflix, Disney+, Apple TV, and Food Network, combining a technical understanding of production systems with the precision and organization required for long-form storytelling.

Designed and coded by Jedy from the ground up, this site features a custom masonry gallery and optimized image rendering built with CSS and JavaScript.
